About

Get to know who I am

Hello, I am Alli Salman Babangida. I am a self-taught frontend developer based in Nigeria. I enjoy using my creativity to build aesthetically pleasing, responsive and clean user-friendly websites.

For over a year and half, I have designed several websites and games to improve my skills. These skills have enabled me to work with diverse web technologies and develop more enthusiasm and passion for frontend development.

When I'm not coding or learning a new technology, I spend my leisure time playing football, video games, watching a movie or reading a book.

Currently i work as a freelancer but I'm open to new opportunities. I like to work in an environment where my skills and expertise will be needed and explored.
